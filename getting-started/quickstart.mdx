## Step 1 — Create an account

Go to [doqo.in/signup](https://doqo.in/signup) and create your free account. No credit card required.

## Step 2 — Create a Knowledge Base

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Knowledge Base">
    From your dashboard, click **Knowledge Base** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new KB">
    Click **+ New Knowledge Base** and give it a name (e.g. "Return Policy", "Product FAQs").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your documents">
    Upload your PDFs, Word docs, or paste plain text. Doqo supports `.pdf`, `.docx`, and `.txt` files.

    <Note>
      Processing usually takes 30–60 seconds depending on file size.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3 — Test your chatbot

Once your documents are indexed (status shows **Ready**), go to **Chat Preview** in the sidebar and ask your bot a question. Try something your customers would actually ask, like:

> "What is your return policy for kurtis?"

## Step 4 — Embed on your website

Go to **Embed** in the sidebar. Copy the script tag and paste it just before `</body>` on your website.

```html
<script
  src="https://doqo.in/widget.js"
  data-kb="YOUR_KB_ID"
  data-color="#E05C3A"
  data-name="Support Bot">
</script>
```

<Tip>
  For platform-specific instructions (Shopify, WordPress, Wix, etc.) see the [Embed Widget](/embed/overview) section.
</Tip>

## Step 5 — Share the chat link

You can also share a direct link to your chatbot — no embed needed:

```
https://doqo.in/chat/YOUR_KB_ID
```

Find this link in the **Embed** dashboard under **Shareable demo link**.

## You're live! 🎉

Your chatbot is now answering customer questions. Check the **Gap Report** in a few days to see which questions it couldn't answer — those are your content gaps to fix.
