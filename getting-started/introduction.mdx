## Overview

Doqo is an AI-powered customer support chatbot built for **Indian D2C brands**. You upload your existing documents — return policies, FAQs, product guides, size charts — and Doqo instantly turns them into a chatbot that answers customer questions 24/7.

Your chatbot can be deployed on:
- Your **website** (via embed widget)
- **WhatsApp** (coming soon)
- A **shareable link** you can send to anyone

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your documents">
    Create a Knowledge Base and upload your PDFs, Word documents, or plain text files. Doqo processes and indexes them automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your bot">
    Set your bot's name, color, and greeting message to match your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy anywhere">
    Copy one line of code to embed on your website, or share the direct chat link with customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor and improve">
    Use the Gap Report to see which questions your bot couldn't answer, then fill those gaps by adding more content.
  </Step>
</Steps>
