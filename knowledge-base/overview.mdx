## What is a Knowledge Base?

A Knowledge Base (KB) is a collection of documents that your chatbot uses to answer questions. When a customer asks something, Doqo searches your KB for the most relevant content and generates an accurate, friendly answer.

You can create **multiple knowledge bases** — one per use case, product line, or brand.

## Creating a Knowledge Base

1. Go to **Dashboard → Knowledge Base**
2. Click **+ New Knowledge Base**
3. Give it a clear name (e.g. "Return Policy", "Product Guide", "FAQs")
4. Upload your documents

## Document statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Processing** | Doqo is reading and indexing your document |
| **Ready** | Document is indexed and your bot can use it |
| **Failed** | Something went wrong — try re-uploading |

<Note>
  Processing takes 30–60 seconds for most documents. Large PDFs (50+ pages) may take up to 2 minutes.
</Note>

## Tips for better results

- **One topic per KB** — Keep return policies separate from product guides. This makes answers more precise.
- **Use clear headings** — Doqo reads the structure of your documents. Well-formatted files give better answers.
- **Keep content updated** — If your policy changes, delete the old file and upload the new one.
