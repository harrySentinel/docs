## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Project Settings">
    In the Webflow Designer, click the gear icon to open **Project Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Custom Code tab">
    Click the **Custom Code** tab at the top of the settings panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Footer Code">
    Scroll down to the **Footer Code** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the script">
    Paste the Doqo script there:

    ```html
    <script
      src="https://doqo.in/widget.js"
      data-kb="YOUR_KB_ID"
      data-color="#E05C3A"
      data-name="Support Bot"
      data-greeting="Hi! How can I help you today?">
    </script>
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Click **Save Changes** then hit **Publish**. Your chatbot is now live on your Webflow site.
  </Step>
</Steps>
