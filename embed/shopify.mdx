## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Shopify admin">
    Log in to your Shopify admin panel and go to **Online Store → Themes**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your theme code">
    Click the three dots `(...)` next to your active theme and select **Edit Code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open theme.liquid">
    In the left sidebar under **Layout**, click on `theme.liquid`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the closing body tag">
    Use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F) to search for `</body>`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the script">
    Paste the Doqo script just above `</body>`:

    ```html
    <script
      src="https://doqo.in/widget.js"
      data-kb="YOUR_KB_ID"
      data-color="#E05C3A"
      data-name="Support Bot"
      data-greeting="Hi! How can I help you today?">
    </script>
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** in the top right. Your chatbot is now live on your store.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can set `data-color` to match your store's brand color for a seamless experience.
</Tip>
